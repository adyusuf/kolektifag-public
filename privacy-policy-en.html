KolektifAğ. — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 5 July 2026

At KolektifAğ ("we", the "Service") we respect your privacy. This policy explains what personal data is collected by the KolektifAğ mobile app and website, how it is used, and what your rights are.

1. Data Controller

KolektifAğ
Contact: info@kolektifag.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information you provide at registration

  • First name, last name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored only as a one-way hash — never kept in plain text)
  • Invitation code (for closed-circuit membership)

2.2 Optional information you may add to your profile

  • Date of birth, place of birth
  • Gender, marital status, nationalities
  • Address details (neighborhood, district, province, country)
  • Education, certificates, organizations you have worked at, occupation
  • Phone numbers, additional email addresses
  • Children and pets
  • Profile photo and cover photo
  • Biography text

Filling in these fields is not mandatory. You alone decide what you share.

2.3 Content you create, not us

  • Posts, comments, events
  • Product/service listings
  • Media galleries and photos
  • Working group and board content
  • Messaging (chat) data

2.4 Technical data collected automatically

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version (iOS / Android)
  • App version
  • IP address (for general geographic location estimation)
  • Error logs and app crash reports
  • Push notification token (FCM / APNs)

3. Purposes for Which We Use Data

  • Account creation, authentication, session management
  • Content sharing and interaction between community members
  • Displaying your content to authorized members (closed-circuit community)
  • Sending push notifications (new event, message, invitation acceptance, etc.)
  • Improving the Service, diagnosing errors
  • Fulfilling legal obligations (KVKK and relevant legislation)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. With Whom We Share Data

KolektifAğ is a closed-circuit community. Your content is viewed only by community members; it is not public.

With the following third-party service providers, only the data required for the function is transmitted:

ServicePurposeData transmitted
Google Firebase Cloud MessagingAndroid push notificationsDevice token, notification text
Apple Push Notification ServiceiOS push notificationsDevice token, notification text
SentryError and crash reportsError traces, device type (contains no personal data)

These service providers are subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention Period

  • As long as the account is active: all profile and content data is retained.
  • Account closure: your data is deleted within 30 days of your request.
  • Legal obligations: despite a deletion request, data may be kept for as long as the law requires it to be retained.
  • Error logs: automatically deleted after 90 days.

6. Your Rights (KVKK Article 11 / GDPR Articles 15-22)

  • Access: Learning which of your data is being processed
  • Rectification: Requesting correction of incorrect/incomplete data
  • Erasure: Requesting deletion of your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Downloading your data in a machine-readable format (JSON) — possible via the in-app "Download My Data" feature
  • Restriction of processing: Objecting to processing for certain purposes
  • Complaint: The right to lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Authority (KVKK) or the competent supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, write to info@kolektifag.com. It will be answered within 30 days.

7. Data Security

  • All connections are encrypted with HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are one-way hashed with the Argon2id algorithm (never kept in plain text).
  • Our servers are hosted on our own infrastructure, in Türkiye (no third-party cloud — AWS/Google Cloud/Azure — is used); database access is restricted.
  • Authorization is handled with JWT; a short-lived access token (15 min) plus a revocable refresh token stored on the server. Social media OAuth tokens are stored encrypted.
  • In the mobile app, sensitive data is stored using Secure Storage (Keychain/Keystore).

Nevertheless, the 100% security of any data transmitted over the internet cannot be guaranteed.

8. Children

KolektifAğ does not provide services to children under the age of 13. If it is determined that a user below this age has created an account, the account is closed immediately.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

  • Web side: the session token is stored in an httpOnly cookie (JavaScript cannot access it, CSRF-protected). We do not use third-party/tracking cookies.
  • Mobile side: the authentication token is stored in the device's secure storage (Secure Storage).

10. International Data Transfer

Your main user data (account, profile, content and messages) is stored on our own server in Türkiye and is not transferred abroad. Only push notification tokens (Google/Apple) and crash/error diagnostic data (Sentry) are processed on the overseas infrastructure of the relevant services. Under Article 9 of the KVKK, by completing your membership you give explicit consent to this limited overseas processing.

11. Changes to This Policy

  • You are notified via an in-app notification
  • The "Last updated" date of this page is renewed
  • An email is sent for significant changes

Changes take effect 30 days after publication. If you do not accept them, you may close your account.
