KolektifAğ Yönetim. — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 5 July 2026

KolektifAğ Yönetim (Project Management) ("we", "the Service") is an independent project management application developed for the board of directors, the audit board and the working units of the KolektifAğ community. This policy explains what personal data the application collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data Controller

KolektifAğ
Contact: info@kolektifag.com

2. Access Condition

KolektifAğ Yönetim is available only to KolektifAğ members and users assigned to at least one unit. There is no public registration flow; your account is tied to your existing KolektifAğ membership.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Information from your KolektifAğ account

  • First name, last name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Your unit/department memberships and positions

3.2 Content you create within the application

  • Projects and work items (issues), their descriptions, status/priority information
  • Task and to-do records, checklists, comments
  • Meeting records (title, agenda, date/time, attendee list)
  • File attachments (documents uploaded to a task/work item)

3.3 Technical data collected automatically

  • Device type and operating system version (iOS / Android)
  • Application version
  • Push notification token (Expo Push Notification Service)
  • Error logs and application crash reports

4. Purposes for Which We Use Data

  • Authentication and session management
  • Displaying project, task and meeting data to the relevant unit members
  • Sending task assignment, status update and reminder notifications
  • Improving the Service and diagnosing errors

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. With Whom We Share Data

Your content is viewed only by KolektifAğ members authorized for the relevant project/unit; it is not public.

ServicePurposeData transmitted
Expo Push Notification ServicePush notificationsDevice push token, notification text

These service providers are subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention Period

  • As long as your account/unit membership is active: your project, task and meeting data is retained.
  • When your unit membership ends: your access is removed; content you created in the past may continue to be retained as part of the unit's records.
  • Error logs: automatically deleted after 90 days.

7. Your Rights (KVKK Article 11 / GDPR Articles 15-22)

  • Access: Learn which of your data is being processed
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate/incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction of processing: Object to processing for certain purposes
  • Complaint: Right to lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Authority (KVKK) or the competent supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, write to info@kolektifag.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

  • All connections are encrypted with HTTPS/TLS.
  • Our servers are hosted on our own infrastructure, in Türkiye (no third-party cloud — AWS/Google Cloud/Azure — is used).
  • Passwords are one-way hashed with Argon2id. Authorization is handled with JWT (short-lived access token + revocable refresh token).
  • In the mobile application, the authentication token is kept in the device's secure storage (Secure Storage).

Nevertheless, no data transmitted over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

9. Children

Due to KolektifAğ's closed, adults-only membership structure, KolektifAğ Yönetim does not provide services to children under the age of 13.

10. Local Storage

The authentication token is kept in the device's secure storage (Keychain/Keystore, and localStorage on the web). We do not use third-party cookies.

11. Changes to This Policy

Significant changes are announced by updating the "Last updated" date on this page and, where necessary, through an in-app notification.

12. Contact

For questions/requests about this policy or your data:

Email: info@kolektifag.com
