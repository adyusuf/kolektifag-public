KolektifAğ. — Account Deletion

Effective date: May 6, 2026

This page explains how to delete your account and personal data in the KolektifAğ app.

⚠️ Account deletion is irreversible. Your profile information, posts, events, comments, and all your content are permanently deleted. You may want to download your data before deleting (explained below).

Method 1 — From within the app (recommended)

  1. Open the KolektifAğ app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Profile icon at the top right.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Close Account".
  4. In the confirmation dialog, press the "Yes, Close" button.

From the moment your request is received:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated (your session is closed on all devices).
  • All your personal data is anonymized within 30 days.
  • For 30 days, in case of an error, mistake, or similar situation, you can request cancellation via info@kolektifag.com.

Method 2 — Request by email

If you do not have access to the app or cannot delete from within the app for any other reason, apply by email:

📧 Write to info@kolektifag.com

When sending the email, please include the following information:

  • Your registered email address (for identity verification)
  • Your full name
  • A clear statement such as "I want to delete my account and my data"

Your request is completed within 30 days. The response is sent to the same email address.

Data to be deleted

Data typeDeletion period
Profile information (name, email, biography, photo)30 days
Posts, comments, likes30 days
Events, products30 days
Messages (chat history)30 days
Push notification tokensImmediately
Session (refresh) tokensImmediately (revoked the moment the request is made)
Error logs (Sentry)90 days (automatic)

Data that may continue to be retained

The following data may be retained in anonymized form due to legal obligations:

  • Legal record-keeping periods (e.g. financial records — contain no personal information)
  • IP / device hash records for fraud / abuse prevention purposes (not linked to personal data)
  • If community members' shared content quotes you, the quote may remain without being linked to personal information

Download your data (before deleting)

Under Article 11 of the KVKK (Turkish Personal Data Protection Law) and Article 20 of the GDPR, you have the right to download your data in a machine-readable format (JSON):

  1. In the app, go to the Profile → Download My Data menu
  2. A JSON file is downloaded to your device (including posts, messages, and profile)
